# Your Data. Your Machine. Your Rules.

**AsymmFlow** is a sovereign, offline-first ERP system purpose-built for trading and distribution companies across India. It runs entirely on your hardware — no cloud dependency, no recurring subscription, no vendor lock-in — with optional multi-device sync when you need it.

Designed for companies serving government departments, public sector undertakings, and large industrial clients who demand airtight traceability, regulatory compliance, and the assurance that their business data never leaves their control.

### Six Integrated Hubs

**Sales Hub** — Full opportunity pipeline management with configurable stage tracking, RFQ processing with multi-product line items, costing sheets with margin enforcement, five quotation document types, PDF generation on company letterhead, one-click costing-to-offer conversion, customer order lifecycle management, win-rate analytics.

**Finance Hub** — P&L and balance sheet dashboards with dynamic year selection, customer invoicing with cryptographic integrity verification, credit note workflows with over-crediting prevention, e-invoicing, tax return data export, bank reconciliation (auto and manual matching), book-bank reconciliation, cheque register lifecycle management, cash position monitoring, FX revaluation, general ledger and journal entries, A/R aging analysis, cash flow projection.

**Operations Hub** — Multi-currency purchase orders with configurable approval thresholds and enforced state machine, goods received notes with QC inspection and serial capture, delivery notes with atomic serial allocation, end-to-end serial traceability from purchase through delivery to customer invoice, warranty tracking, supplier invoice 3-way matching, shipment management.

**Relationships Hub** — 360-degree customer and supplier profiles with unified KPI dashboards, year-over-year performance filtering, multi-contact management, supplier invoice verification with visual match indicators, automated customer deduplication, configurable customer grading.

**Intelligence Hub** — Butler AI for natural language queries across your entire dataset, OCR document analysis with drag-and-drop, conversation persistence, interactive data visualisations with entity relationship mapping, predictive win probability scoring, automated PDF report generation.

**Administration** — License-based activation with device binding, user and role management with complete audit trail, application settings, cloud sync configuration, automated database backup with integrity verification on every startup.

- **Graceful Shutdown**: All operations complete before close — zero data loss risk

- **Log Management**: Automatic rotation, credential sanitisation, archived history

### Why AsymmFlow

**vs. Enterprise ERPs (SAP / Oracle NetSuite)**
6-8x lower three-year cost of ownership. No implementation consultants required. No mandatory cloud infrastructure. The same core capabilities — invoicing, procurement, traceability, reconciliation, reporting — without the enterprise overhead.

**vs. Mid-Market (Odoo)**
Comparable feature coverage at lower TCO. The decisive advantage is architecture: Odoo requires a server, a database administrator, module configuration, and ongoing hosting. AsymmFlow runs as a single binary on any office laptop.

**vs. Tally Prime**
Same price range, fundamentally different capability. Tally lacks end-to-end serial traceability, AI-powered queries, costing sheet automation, and role-based access control. AsymmFlow is what Tally would be if it were rebuilt for modern Indian distribution.
